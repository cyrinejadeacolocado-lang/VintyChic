About VintyChic

Hi! I’m Cyrine Jade Aliposa Colocado — or you can call me Jade. I created VintyChic to combine my love for fashion and sustainability.

Our Mission

To offer curated ukay-ukay and reworked pieces that are affordable, classy, and eco-friendly. We carefully clean, inspect, and style every item so you get quality secondhand fashion.

About Our Products

From vintage denim and statement dresses to cozy knits and reworked tops — each piece is chosen for durability and charm. We also offer small reworks like cropping, embroidery, and gentle tailoring.
